Study Strategies for ICT Success

Scoring an 'A' in ICT requires both understanding concepts and practical application. Here are proven strategies from our top students:

Practical First Approach

Start with hands-on practice before theory. Our students who first experiment with software tools develop deeper understanding.

Consistent Revision

Students who review material weekly retain 80% more than those who cram before exams.

Doubt Clearing

Top performers ask questions immediately when confused, preventing knowledge gaps.

Our Student Results

In 2023, 78% of our regular students achieved 'A' grades, with 92% scoring above 'B'.

Weekly Study Plan Recommendation

  • Monday: Review previous week's concepts (30 min)
  • Wednesday: Practice practical tasks (45 min)
  • Friday: Attempt past paper questions (1 hour)
  • Sunday: Attend live class & clarify doubts (1.5 hours)
